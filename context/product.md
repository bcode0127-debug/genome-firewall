# Genome Firewall — Product Definition

USER: Infectious-disease clinician treating a suspected Klebsiella
infection, waiting on susceptibility results.

PROBLEM: Lab testing takes 1-3 days. Until it returns, treatment is
a guess. A wrong guess costs the patient time and drives resistance.

SOLUTION: Genome in, per-drug verdict out in minutes — with
calibrated confidence, supporting genes, and an honest no-call.

AI ROLE: Predicting. Per-drug classification with abstention. Not
generating, not summarizing. Every result requires lab confirmation.

IMPACT: The patient starts an effective antibiotic days sooner. The hospital cuts unnecessary broad-spectrum use. Public-health teams see resistance spreading earlier. Every result is decision support, confirmed by standard lab testing.
